Get the attributes of an IFC entity from its guid.

<p align="center">
  <img src="/img/ifc_get_info_by_guid.png" />
</p>

## Input
1. **Model***: the IfcOpenShell model.

2. **Guid***: the guid to look for.

\* Required

## Output
1. **Keyes**: the attributes keyes.

2. **Values**: the attributes values.
